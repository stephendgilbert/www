Procdural Decomposition with DrawEggs

In Section 5.1—Writing Procedures, you learned how to break a single main() function into a series of procedures, eliminating redundancy. Then, in H12—Draw Shapes, you completed a homework assignment doing the same thing.

Let's see if you can do that on your own by writing a complete Java console program named DrawEggs that prints the pattern shown on the right. (Click it to see a larger image).

Here are the requirements:

  • You should use at least at least three procedures, (not counting main()).
  • Do not do any printing inside your main() method. The only thing that main() should contain is procedure calls.
