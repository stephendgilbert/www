Objects, Fonts and Colors


For our final lab exercise today, we're going to practice with creating objects and sending messages. To do that, I'm going to have you work with ahother Java applet, adding fonts and colors.

DIY Business Card

Now that you know how to change colors and how to use different fonts, complete the applet named BusinessCard that you'll find in the Week02 folder. Here are the requirements.

  • Add your name, a title and your email address.
  • The data should fit on a standard 3.5" x 2" business card. (I've added a method to automatically size the window when run in DrJava.)
  • Use at least two different fonts and two different colors.
  • Change the background color in the init() method, not in the paint() method.
