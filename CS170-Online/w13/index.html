Week 13— November 17 to November 23
Arrays and Collections

Welcome to CS 170 Week 13. This week we'll return to the topic of arrays and collections. You'll learn how to process arrays of objects, partially-filled and multi-dimensional array, as well as how to use the ArrayList class. Along the way you'll have some fun manipulating cards and images.

This week you'll learn:

  • how to use arrays that store objects
  • algorithms to process partially-filled arrays
  • how to use arrays as instance variables
  • how to use the generic ArrayList class
  • how to write programs using 2D arrays
  • how to process command-line arguments and write variable-parameter methods

To the right are links to the on-campus handouts and the online course materials for this week. Along with the lessons here, you should finish reading Chapter 7 in your textbook. The Chapter 7 quiz will be given during class next week.

In addition to the reading, you'll need to complete the lab and homework exercises and submit them by Noon on Tuesday, November 25.

By then you should:

  • listen to each of the online lessons.
  • complete and submit the ic19 online lab exercises.
  • complete and submit the Week 13 homework assignments.

We will not have any homework assignments or online lectures next week (because of the Thanksgiving Holiday). There will be class, however, with the Chapter 7 quiz.
